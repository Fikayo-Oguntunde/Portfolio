What is StudyMatch?

StudyMatch is a networking application focusing on peer-to-peer academic collaboration.

It helps university students match with study partners to foster a more effective learning environment.

Why build StudyMatch?

Im an individual who struggles with social anxiety. Although I have good one on one communication skills, I lack the courage to reach out to others when Im in need of help. I realised a lot of people struggle with this also, especially university students.

Thats why im creating StudyMatch, to help university students like myself, to network and meet new people outside their social network.

StudyMatch will help students match with other students with similar interests whether that be for recreational purposes, for co-op opportunities, networking or studying together!
