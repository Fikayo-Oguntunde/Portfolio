Description

An open differential gearbox is a simple mechanical assembly that allows divided engine torque between a vehicle's driven wheels while also allowing them to rotate at different speeds.

My gearbox is composed of the following bevel gears

  1. A large driven bevel gear connected to the left wheel axle
  2. Two internal spider gears that connect the wheel axle gears together. They also spin on their axes allowing for differential speed of the wheels
  3. The wheel axel bevel gears
